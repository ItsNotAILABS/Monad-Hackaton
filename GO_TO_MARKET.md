## Integration Architecture

### What CyberSecurity-AI Adds to MonadBuilder+

#### 1. **Security Intelligence in the IDE**
```
User: "Build a trading bot for MON/USDC with 2x leverage"
  ↓
Studio generates contract proposal
  ↓
THESIS policy kernel checks: "Is this lawful?"
  ↓
CyberSecurity-AI agent intelligence: 
  - Who needs to approve this? (career_get)
  - What does "trader" mean in this org? (career_search)
  - What are the right capability boundaries? (capability_matrix)
  - Generate an approval checklist (demo_packet)
  ↓
IDE shows: "This needs CFO + Risk approval. Here's the audit trail template."
```

#### 2. **Role-Based Governance**
```
THESIS Policy:
  - Trading Agent can execute if: slippage_bps <= 50 && leverage <= 2x
  
CyberSecurity-AI Career:
  - "Trader" role ⇒ skill: risk_assessment, approval: CFO, audit_owner: compliance
  
Result: Agent respects both risk AND role boundaries
```

#### 3. **Incident Response Workflows**
```
Scenario: Agent position loses 10% in 5 minutes
  ↓
THESIS detects policy violation (leverage spikes to 3.2x)
  ↓
CyberSecurity-AI routes incident:
  - Immediate escalation to: Risk Officer
  - Full incident briefing from: Incident Tabletop workflow
  - RACI assignment from: Role taxonomy
  ↓
IDE shows: "Incident detected. RACI teams assigned. Recovery checklist ready."
```

#### 4. **Compliance & Audit Dashboard**
- Live capability matrix: What agents can do, what roles approved it
- Evidence checklist: Automated audit prep for each major transaction
- Control-owner map: Who owns what risk, what's the SLA for remediation
- Market packets: Ready-made security briefings for board/regulators
