# Sales & Customer Acquisition Strategy

## Quick Overview

**Target**: $100K MRR by Q1 2025 (12-16 weeks)  
**Method**: Strategic pilots + viral loop in crypto community  
**Sales Motion**: Both direct (enterprise) and self-serve (crypto-native)

---

## Segment-Specific Sales Strategies

### Segment 1: Enterprise DeFi Teams ($50K-$500K/year)

**Target Companies**:
- Jump Trading, Wintermute, Bankless DAO
- OpenAI (agents team), Anthropic (autonomous capabilities)
- Ethereum Foundation, Polygon, Arbitrum teams
- Block (formerly Square), Ripple, Coinbase

**Value Prop**:
> "Move from 6-month dApp development cycles to 6-week. Built with governance so you pass audits on day 1, not month 6."

**Sales Process**:
1. **Identify**: Look for companies announcing Monad support or new agent capabilities
2. **Introduce**: "We've helped [Reference] go from idea to deployed dApp in 3 weeks. We've built governance into the platform so it works for both speed and compliance."
3. **Pilot**: Free 4-week pilot (limited to 1 dApp, basic features)
4. **Convert**: After success, move to Tier 2 ($2K-$5K/month)

**Proof Points Needed**:
- Case study: First paid customer (25 hours of work with us)
- Video: Studio → deployment in 5 minutes
- Testimonial: "We saved 4 months and $200K in audit costs"

**Timeline**:
- Week 1: Identify 10 targets
- Week 2: Intro calls
- Week 3-6: Pilots (4 companies)
- Week 7: Convert first 2 pilots
- **Target**: 3-5 enterprise pilots by end of month

## Customer Acquisition Funnel

### Phase 1: Awareness (Weeks 1-4)

**Tactics**:
1. **Content Marketing**
   - Blog: "How to Deploy DeFi in Weeks Not Months" (Segment 1)
   - Blog: "Role-Based Governance for AI Agents" (Segment 2)
   - Blog: "How Regulators Actually Think About Blockchain" (Segment 3)
   - LinkedIn posts from founder about each use case

2. **Community Activation**
   - Monad Discord: Announce MonadBuilder+ + security
   - Crypto Twitter: @Monad, @zkSync, @Polygon, @ElizaOS communities
   - Security Twitter: @CISOs, @SecuritySummit, @RSAConference hashtags
   - Reddit: r/defi, r/CyberSecurity, r/blockchain, r/LLMs

3. **Product Hunt Launch**
   - Post MonadBuilder+ (full stack) + mention CyberSecurity-AI integration
   - Target: 1K+ upvotes, 5K+ views, 50+ comments
   - Leverage crypto community for initial traction

4. **Podcast / Speaking**
   - Reach out to 10 crypto/AI/security podcasts
   - Book 2-3 speaking slots at conferences (Defcon, ETHDenver, SBF)

**Target Metrics**:
- 1K sign-ups to waitlist
- 500 active Discord/community members
- 100+ demo video views
- 20+ inbound leads

### Phase 2: Engagement (Weeks 5-8)

**Tactics**:
1. **Pilot Programs**
   - Segment 1 (DeFi): 4 free pilots
   - Segment 2 (Security): 2 free POCs
   - Segment 3 (Regulated): 1 compliance assessment
   - Segment 4 (AI): 1 partnership exploration

2. **Content Deepdives**
   - Webinar: "Building Auditable dApps in 3 Weeks" (Segment 1)
   - Webinar: "Role-Based Governance for AI Agents" (Segment 2)
   - Webinar: "Compliance + Speed: How MonadBuilder+ Solves the Blockchain Paradox" (Segment 3)
   - Webinar: "Embedding Security into Agent Platforms" (Segment 4)

3. **Customer Stories**
   - Early pilots → case study interviews
   - Target: 3 customer stories (short, 1-page each)

4. **Product Visibility**
   - GitHub trending (make CyberSecurity-AI integration prominent)
   - Hacker News: Post integration announcement (target 1st page)
   - LLM aggregators: Add to OpenAI directory, Anthropic showcase

**Target Metrics**:
- 8-10 pilots in progress
- 2K+ webinar registrations
- 3 customer stories drafted
- 200+ inbound leads

### Phase 3: Conversion (Weeks 9-12)

**Tactics**:
1. **Pilot Conversion**
   - Close first 3-5 pilots into Tier 2 contracts
   - Target: $5K-$20K/month each

2. **Case Study Launch**
   - Publish 3 customer stories (blog + PDF)
   - Video testimonials (2-3 minutes each)

3. **Sales Outreach**
   - Direct outreach to 50 warm leads (from pilots, community)
   - Use case templates: "This is what we built for [Segment]"

4. **Partnership Announcements**
   - Monad Foundation partnership (cross-promote)
   - Anthropic partnership (if applicable)
   - Cloud vendor partnership (Render, Railway)

**Target Metrics**:
- 3-5 Tier 2 contracts ($5K-$20K/month each)
- $20K-$100K MRR
- 10+ customer stories in circulation
- 500+ inbound leads

### Phase 4: Acceleration (Weeks 13-16)

**Tactics**:
1. **Enterprise Contracts**
   - Close 1-2 Tier 3 contracts ($50K-$500K/year)
   - Target: Enterprise DeFi + regulated market

2. **Self-Serve Growth**
   - Launch freemium tier
   - Target: 1K free users
   - 10% conversion to Tier 2 = 100 customers

3. **Industry Recognition**
   - Award submissions (Best AI Platform, Best Security Tool)
   - Analyst briefings (Gartner, Forrester, IDC)

4. **Scale Operations**
   - Hire sales engineer + customer success manager
   - Build customer support infrastructure

**Target Metrics**:
- $100K MRR
- 50+ enterprise pilots
- 10+ Tier 2 customers
- 1-2 Tier 3 customers
- 5K free users

## Win/Loss Strategy

### Win (What We Look For)
- Customer has pain in [Segment] area
- Budget already allocated or available
- Decision maker is technical + business-aware
- Timeline is weeks, not months
- Willing to be a reference after success

### Loss (When to Move On)
- No budget in next 3 months
- Decision-making is by committee (slows us down)
- Expecting free consulting (not pilots)
- Comparing us to low-end alternatives (not our market)
- Timing is 6+ months away

## Metrics Dashboard

Track weekly:
- **Awareness**: Inbound leads, community mentions, content views
- **Engagement**: Pilots in progress, webinar registrations, customer conversations
- **Conversion**: Closed deals, MRR, customer acquisition cost
- **Retention**: Expansion revenue, churn rate, NPS

**Target KPIs**:
- Pilot-to-paid conversion: 50%+ (3 of 5-8 pilots → customers)
- CAC payback: < 6 months
- Net retention rate: 120%+ (expansion revenue)
- Annual contract value (ACV): $5K-$500K range
