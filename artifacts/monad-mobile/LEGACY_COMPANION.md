# Legacy NOVA Companion Prototype

`artifacts/monad-mobile` is retained as a Replit-oriented React Native/Expo prototype. It is **not** a third public product and is not part of the current launch requirement.

The canonical public products are:

1. **MonadBuilder+** — web/browser creation and ecosystem surface.
2. **THESIS Agent Desktop** — sovereign local execution and control surface.

NOVA remains an internal runtime and paired-device capability within the THESIS operating system. A later companion route or PWA may expose only bounded controls such as desktop pairing, connection status, approval or denial of sensitive actions, receipt viewing, device revocation, and emergency bridge disconnect.

No wallet keys, unrestricted execution authority, or silent trading authority belong in this prototype.
